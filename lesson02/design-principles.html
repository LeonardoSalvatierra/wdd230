RULE OF THIRDS

Montagne

www.montagne.com.ar

Montagne's website is like a friendly guide using the Rule of Thirds, where the top part showcases a big, awesome picture representing the main thing, the middle part highlights trends and popular searches for quick attention, and the bottom part displays all the cool products, creating a user-friendly and organized layout that feels like a fun tour through their online world.

WHITE SPACE AND CLEAN DESIGN

Microsoft

www.microsoft.com

Microsoft's website embraces a clean and minimalist design, using ample white space, a cohesive color palette, and strategic spacing between elements to create a visually appealing and user-friendly online experience.

CONTRAST

Compra Gamer

www.compragamer.com

Compra Gamer employs the contrast design principle by showcasing bold differences between elements, using vibrant colors and distinct visual styles, ensuring a dynamic and visually striking presentation that captures attention and enhances user engagement.
